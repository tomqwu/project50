# Store assets checklist

The complete asset requirements for the **project50** App Store and Play Store
listings — icons, screenshots, and graphics with their exact specs.

> **This doc lists requirements only.** The assets themselves (icon artwork,
> rendered screenshots, feature graphic) are produced separately under **#95**
> (iOS) and **#113** (Android). Use this as the spec sheet those tasks build to,
> and as the upload checklist for `IOS-SUBMISSION.md` and `ANDROID-SUBMISSION.md`.

The app currently ships placeholder assets: `apps/mobile/app.json` references
`./assets/icon.png` and a splash with `backgroundColor: #121013`; the Android
adaptive icon has only a `backgroundColor` and **no `foregroundImage`**. Brand
colors in use: charcoal `#121013`, accent `#d6ff3f` (see `apps/mobile/src/theme.ts`
and the web brand). Dark UI (`userInterfaceStyle: dark`).

## 2. iOS App Store listing assets (#95)

Uploaded in App Store Connect (see `IOS-SUBMISSION.md` §2).

### App Store icon

- **1024 × 1024** PNG, no alpha, no transparency, sRGB. (Pulled from the build's
  icon, but Connect may also require/show the marketing 1024.)

### Screenshots — required device sizes

Apple requires the largest size per supported device family. The app sets
`ios.supportsTablet: true`, so **iPad screenshots are required** unless tablet
support is turned off first.

| Device family | Pixel size (portrait) | Required? | Count |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| iPhone 6.9" (16/15 Pro Max class) | **1320 × 2868** | **Yes** | 3–10 |
| iPhone 6.7" (alt to 6.9") | 1290 × 2796 | accepted in lieu | 3–10 |
| iPhone 6.5" (legacy) | 1242 × 2688 | optional if 6.9 given | up to 10 |
| iPad Pro 13" | **2064 × 2752** | **Yes if supportsTablet** | 3–10 |
| iPad Pro 12.9" (alt) | 2048 × 2732 | accepted in lieu | 3–10 |

- Landscape variants are the transposed dimensions; portrait is the app's
  orientation (`orientation: portrait` in `app.json`), so ship portrait.

### App preview (optional)

## 4. Shared brand notes for asset production

- Colors: background/charcoal `#121013`, accent `#d6ff3f`, light text `#f2f0ec`.
- Dark theme throughout; screenshots should reflect the dark UI.
- Keep marketing copy on screenshots consistent with the listing (no claims the
  app doesn't deliver; e.g. only show Google sign-in if it ships).
- No third-party logos beyond what's permitted (Facebook/Google sign-in button
  branding must follow each provider's brand guidelines).
